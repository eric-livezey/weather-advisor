Frequently Asked Questions

  • Currently Weather Advisor only supports the top 10 most populous northeastern cities. These cities are as follows:

    • New York City, New York
    • Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
    • Hempstead, New York
    • Washington, District of Columbia
    • Boston, Massachusetts
    • Baltimore, Maryland
    • Brookhaven, New York
    • Virginia Beach, Virginia
    • Islip, New York
    • Newark, New Jersey

  • Precipitation is calculated using the Brier score.

  • Observed data is collected from the National Weather Service. Due to various issues with automatically recording certain data, Weather stations unfortunately do not collect every single statistic every single hour (especially in the case of precipitition). Thus, observed data is occasionally not present.

    In the case of forecast data, it is mostly dependent on the data provided by each individual service. It is rare, but occasionally services will not provide all necessary data for specific times. It is also true that this site is self-hosted and thus has not maintained complete 100% uptime thus far. In the case that there is a large period where data is not present, it is likely because the server was offline and unable to collect it.
