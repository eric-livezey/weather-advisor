Frequently Asked Questions

What Locations are Available?

Currently Weather Advisor only supports the top 10 most populous northeastern cities. These cities are as follows:

  • New York City, New York
  • Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Hempstead, New York
  • Washington, District of Columbia
  • Boston, Massachusetts
  • Baltimore, Maryland
  • Brookhaven, New York
  • Virginia Beach, Virginia
  • Islip, New York
  • Newark, New Jersey

How is precipitation accuracy measured?

Precipitation is calculated using the Brier score.
